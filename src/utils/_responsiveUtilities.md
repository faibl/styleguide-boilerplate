### Scss Media Query Breakpoint variables

Commonly used media query conditions exposed as scss variables:
`$sgbp-tiny-only`
`$sgbp-tiny-up`
`$sgbp-small-only`
`$sgbp-small-up`
`$sgbp-medium-only
`$sgbp-medium-up`
`$sgbp-large-only`
`$sgbp-large-up`
