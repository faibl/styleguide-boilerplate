## Col

### Col (default)

Cols have a defined right margin

    @example
    <div class="sgbp-col">
        Col 1
    </div>
    <div class="sgbp-col">
        Col 2
    </div>
    <div class="sgbp-col">
        Col 3
    </div>

### Col (with separator)
  
    @example
    <div class="sgbp-col sgbp-col--withSeparator">
        Col 1
    </div>
    <div class="sgbp-col sgbp-col--withSeparator">
        Col 2
    </div>
    <div class="sgbp-col sgbp-col--withSeparator">
        Col 3
    </div>
