## Clickable

### Clickable (Default)

Changes the cursor to "pointer" on hovering 

    @example
    <div class="sgbp-clickable">
        Clickable
    </div>

### Clickable (Touchy)

Slightly increases the touchable area  

    @example
    <div class="sgbp-clickable sgbp-clickable--touchy">
        Clickable
    </div>
